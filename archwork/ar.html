AR Overlays

In collaboration with Daniel Flinchbaugh

Following a series of oil paintings of the dilapidated urban industrial landscape, Daniel and I began collaborating on an augmented reality extension of his work. One day after that discussion, I had these prototypes going. These use Vuforia in the Unity game engine to track key points on the surface to place the overlays in position. I created some speculative work looking at including animated 3D overlays as well.
