Scalar Playground

This design is about scale. The project works to fit density into the Brooklyn landscape by playing with the occupation at the urban, block, and building scales. The program includes residential towers, artist studios, and commercial space. The spatial play is reinforced through the use of graffiti and projected art that both have diverse interactions based on scale and proximity. The site is surrounded by low-lying industrial buildings and faces the East River.
