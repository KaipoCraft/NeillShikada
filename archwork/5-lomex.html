LOM-X-R

This is a virtual reality walkthrough of the unbuilt Lower Manhattan Expressway (LOMEX); a large-scale infrastructure project proposed by Robert Moses and designed by Paul Rudolph. This Brutalist, speculative design was seen as a way to construct a highway through Manhattan without disrupting access between the north and south. Bringing the highway below grade allowed Rudolph to place large, modular housing developments above, which would also include a public transportation system reminiscent of Walt Disney’s “PeopleMovers”
