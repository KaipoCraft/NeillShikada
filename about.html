About

I am a studying Creative Technology and Design at the ATLAS Institute at CU Boulder. With my background in architecture and movement, I'm focusing my studies on the place of motion and space in tech.

When I'm not coding or moving, you'll find me painting in the mountains, running through the foothills, or cooking for my co-op.

If you'd like to chat about the untapped potential of our bodies in tech or go people-watching, feel free to email me
