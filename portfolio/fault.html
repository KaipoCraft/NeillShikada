Communal Urban Fault

This project is about community. The city of Charleston struggles with a lack of 3rd places and softer street edges. This project seeks to create a communal center that breaks with the urban fabric of the city. The center includes a pub, Irish dance studio, a theater, and open, public space. There are two inverted itineraries on either side of the site - one for the performers; the other for the public. These both frame and meet at the open air theater.
