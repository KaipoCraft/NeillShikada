Urban Gateway

This project uses the urban landscape of Savannah as the driver of form. The site along the Riverwalk presents an opportunity to disrupt the harsh edge of buildings separating the Riverwalk from the rest of the city. This design breaks the wall of buildings by opening the bottom and using spatial folding to create a link between the river and city.
