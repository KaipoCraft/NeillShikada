This arch can be seen several times in West Philadelphia. Rowhomes are the archetypal residential architecture of Philadelphia, mostly built in the early 1900's. These designs were often repeated on a block by block scale and their beauty is only matched by their use of flair and ornament. This arch - visible from my backyard - is a simple representative of that ornament.

Oil on canvas 8 x 10
