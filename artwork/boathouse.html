Boathouse Row

Boathouse Row is one of the most recognizable Philadelphia monuments. But as a former rower myself, I always relish the chance to pass by it and look longingly at the shells, oars, and launches coming off of its shores. I was fortunate enough to row on this river (the Schyulkill) when I was in college on my first trip to Philadelphia. It was when I fell in love with the city.

Oil on canvas 8 x 10
