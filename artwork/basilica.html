Basilica Palladiana

From a memory, the world before COVID. I lived in Vicenza, Italy for 4 months in 2018. I was able to pick up some Italian, travel to places I never thought I would see, and feel an amount of freedom I have been seeking ever since. This basilica is the central wayfinding monument of the city. Built while under Venetian rule, the roof is actually an upside-down boat hull. Leveraging their specialities to be as efficient as possible is a trait that I seek to emmulate in my life.

Oil on canvas 8 x 10
